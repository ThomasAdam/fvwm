GotoDeskAndPage

   --------------------------------------------------------------------------

   GotoDeskAndPage prev | desk xpage ypage

   Switches the current viewport to another desktop and page, similar to the
   GotoDesk and GotoPage commands. The new desk is desk and the new page is
   (xpage,ypage).

   --------------------------------------------------------------------------

   fvwm 2.6.3 (from cvs)
