RefreshWindow

   --------------------------------------------------------------------------

   RefreshWindow

   Causes the chosen window to redraw itself. All pending updates of the
   window's style and look are applied immediately. E.g. if Style or
   TitleStyle commands were issued inside a fvwm function.

   --------------------------------------------------------------------------

   fvwm 2.6.3 (from cvs)
