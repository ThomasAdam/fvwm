Refresh

   --------------------------------------------------------------------------

   Refresh

   Causes all windows on the screen to redraw themselves. All pending updates
   of all windows' styles and looks are applied immediately. E.g. if Style or
   TitleStyle commands were issued inside a fvwm function.

   --------------------------------------------------------------------------

   fvwm 2.6.3 (from cvs)
