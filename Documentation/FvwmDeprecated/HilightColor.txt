HilightColor

   --------------------------------------------------------------------------

   HilightColor textcolor backgroundcolor

   This command is obsoleted by the Style options HilightFore and
   HilightBack. Please use

 Style * HilightFore textcolor, HilightBack backgroundcolor

   instead.

   --------------------------------------------------------------------------
