UnsetEnv

   --------------------------------------------------------------------------

   UnsetEnv [variable]

   Unset an environment variable, similar to shell's export or unsetenv
   command. The variable then is removed from the environment array inherited
   by processes started directly by fvwm.

   --------------------------------------------------------------------------

   fvwm 2.6.3 (from cvs)
