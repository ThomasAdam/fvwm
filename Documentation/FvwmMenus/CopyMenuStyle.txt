CopyMenuStyle

   --------------------------------------------------------------------------

   CopyMenuStyle orig-menustyle dest-menustyle

   Copy orig-menustyle to dest-menustyle, where orig-menustyle is an existing
   menu style. If the menu style dest_menustyle does not exist, then it is
   created.

   --------------------------------------------------------------------------
