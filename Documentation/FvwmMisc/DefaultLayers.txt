DefaultLayers

   --------------------------------------------------------------------------

   DefaultLayers bottom put top

   Changes the layers that are used for the StaysOnBottom, StaysPut,
   StaysOnTop Style options. Initially, the layers 2, 4 and 6 are used.

   --------------------------------------------------------------------------

   fvwm 2.6.3 (from cvs)
