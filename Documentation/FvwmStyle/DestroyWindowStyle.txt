DestroyWindowStyle

   --------------------------------------------------------------------------

   DestroyWindowStyle

   deletes the styles set by the WindowStyle command on the selected window.
   The changes take effect immediately.

   --------------------------------------------------------------------------

   fvwm 2.6.3 (from cvs)
