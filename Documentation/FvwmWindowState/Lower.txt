Lower

   --------------------------------------------------------------------------

   Lower

   Allows the user to lower a window. Note that this lowers a window only in
   its layer. To bring a window to the absolute bottom, use

 AddToFunc lower-to-bottom
  + I Layer 0 0
  + I Lower

   --------------------------------------------------------------------------

   fvwm 2.6.3 (from cvs)
