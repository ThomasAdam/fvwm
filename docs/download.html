Old releases of fvwm1 and fvwm2 and (for the brave), the latest alpha and beta releases as well as unofficial patches can be found here.

Please keep in mind that the Beta code can potentially become unstable or buggy at any time, so USE AT YOUR OWN RISK. Of course you may find bugs in "Official" versions also. If you do download a Beta version, we do appreciate bug reports. Send bug reports to the FVWM Workers Mailing List.

If you just have a question, be sure to first read our Documentation first, then you might want to try our Mailing List.

I know you have heard this a million times...always tell us what release of fvwm you are running, your operating system, and other pertinent information.

Development right now (11/98) is quite active, expect to see a new release "Real Soon Now".
